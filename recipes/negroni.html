Description

The Negroni is an italian classic. Born in the 1860s as a riff on another Italian classic called the Americano, this cocktail spread from italy and is now a classic all over the world.

A negroni is a simple pleasure of a complex life. A simple, classy but humble drink to order any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • 1.5oz Gin

  • 3/4oz Sweet vermouth

  • 3/4oz Campari

Steps

  1. Step 1: Fill cocktail mixing glass with ice

  2. Step 2: Pour gin, sweet vermouth and campari over the ice

  3. Step 3: Stir cocktail thoroughly, around 10 seconds or until mixing glass begins to frost

  4. Step 4: Strain cocktail over ice or serve up

  5. Step 5: Enjoy!
