Description

The industry sour is new in the genus of cocktail histories, created by Ted Kilgore in 2014. Comprised of controversial ingredients, the cocktail is an unexpected treat for Chartreuse and Fernet-Branca haters.

With all equal pours, the cocktail is easy to remember and great to keep on deck for any bartender that aims to impress a curious guest.

Ingredients

  • 1oz Fernet-Branca

  • 1oz Green Chartreuse

  • 1oz Simple syrup

  • 1oz Lime juice

Steps

  1. Step 1: Fill cocktail shaker with ice

  2. Step 2: Pour Green Chartreuse, Fernet-Branca, simple syrup and lime juice over the ice

  3. Step 3: Shake cocktail thoroughly, around 10 seconds or until cocktail shaker begins to frost

  4. Step 4: Strain cocktail over ice or serve up

  5. Step 5: Enjoy!
